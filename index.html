Rules:

  1. Roll the dice for your turn.
  2. Continue rolling the die until you're happy with the number of points you've scored.
    Then, "hold" by handing the die over to your opponent, and track all points earned during your turn.
  3. In case you hit '1' you loose your points for that round, and the turn goes to your opponent.
  4. Reach 100 points first and win the game.
